✅ Do’s
Think before posting — ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.
Protect your privacy — use strong passwords and adjust privacy settings.
Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
Be respectful in interactions — engage positively and avoid online conflicts.
Use social media productively — follow educational pages, networks, and opportunities.
❌ Don’ts
Don’t share personal or sensitive information (e.g., address, passwords, financial details).
Don’t engage in cyberbullying or trolling — it can harm others and your reputation.
Don’t overshare — too much personal content can affect your professional image.
Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content — it may impact future career prospects.
Don’t believe or spread rumors without checking facts.
