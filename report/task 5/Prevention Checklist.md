✅ UPI Fraud Prevention Checklist
Do’s
✔️ Always verify the receiver’s name before sending money
✔️ Use official UPI apps (like bank apps or trusted platforms only)
✔️ Check transaction messages carefully before approving
✔️ Enable app lock / biometric security for your payment apps
✔️ Report suspicious activity immediately to bank or helpline (1930)
❌ Don’ts
❌ Don’t scan QR codes to receive money
❌ Don’t share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone
❌ Don’t click on unknown payment links
❌ Don’t trust unknown callers pretending to be bank officials
❌ Don’t rush into transactions under pressure or urgency
